Leapstart Programme from FITKIDS
Fitkids is engaged in developing physical education curriculum and training programmes for school children and conducting physical education classes and sports training workshops in schools using the Leapstart programme. Fitkids has developed the Leapstart programme as an exclusive licensee of the internationally-acclaimed SPARK (Sports, play and Active Recreation for kids) method.

MAHARISHI VIDYA MANDIR MATRICULATION HIGHER SECONDARY SCHOOL hereby engages Fitkids to conduct the following programme (“Leapstart Programme”) during the period and for the grades LKG to 12.

Physical education and training programmes based on Internationally acclaimed SPARK (Sports, Play and Active Recreation for Kids) method.
