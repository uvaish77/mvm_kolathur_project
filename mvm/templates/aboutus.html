About us


Maharishi Vidya Mandir Group of schools from the year June 2020 world renowned Maharishi Vidya Mandir, Which have been passionately involved in inspiring young minds since 1983 with a vision ‘Knowledge is structured in Consciousness’. The schools have been providing outstanding education through committed and highly qualified Teaching Staff. Our consistently updated curriculum, advanced technology enabled teaching methods and personalized attention ensures that our kids receive the right dose of education, attitude, inspiration, problem-solving abilities, communication skills and knowledge.


Come to the home of knowledge – The Gurukul; and be sure of your kids’ success in the highly competitive education scenario.
