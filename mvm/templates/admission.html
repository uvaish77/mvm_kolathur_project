ADMISSION FOR CLASSES LKG TO IX & XI


INSTRUCTIONS:


1.It is online application. Please do not print the registration form.
2.Please fill in the details and click submit.
3.It is only online application for registration and does not confer any right for admission.
4.Only those who receive official communication need to appear for certificates registration with duly filled in forms and photographs.
5.No enquiry regarding admission either in persons or through phone shall be entertained.
6.Birth certificates shall be verified with the respective local bodies and if any discrepancy is found admission will be cancelled.
7.Admission is always subject to availability of sent.
8.If selected, you need to furnish the following certificates in originals and Xerox.
9.Birth certificate
10.Community certificate
11.Income certificate
12.Address proof i) Aadhar card ii) Ration card
13.Public are categorically in formed that no agency or individual other than the school management is entrusted with admission and anyone acting on others representations, assurances shall do so at his/her risk and the school shall not be in any way held liable in this regard.
14.For admission in LKG a child should have completed 3 (three) years of age as on 31st July.
15.Transfer certificate is a must for admission to classes UKG to IX and XI std.


CORRESPONDENT.


ELIGIBILITY FOR ADMISSION TO PLUS ONE


Candidates should have passed S.S.L.C Examination or other equivalent examination in English medium which is recognized by the Govt. of Tamilnadu. The admission are made only on merit basis and on priority of registration. Registration does not guarantee a seat for plus one Registered students will be called for an interview for evaluation of general standard.
Application form and prospectus can be obtained from the school office on payment of Rs. 100/-


DOCUMENTS TO BE SUBMITTED AT THE TIME OF INTERVIEW:


Original Mark Sheet of Std. X (S.S.L.C. Examination or equivalent examination)
2.Xerox copy of Birth Certificate
3.Transfer Certificate (Original)
4.Conduct Certificate (Original)
5.A Xerox copy of the community certificate
6.Passport size photos 4 Nos
